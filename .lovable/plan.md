

# Professional Medical Bandages Website

## Overview
A premium, modern website for a medical cotton bandages company featuring immersive animations, 3D elements, and full content management capabilities. The design will emphasize trust, quality, and professionalism with a clean blue & white medical aesthetic.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation & Design System

### Core Setup
- **Color Palette**: Medical blue (#0066CC), white, light blue accents, with navy for headers
- **Typography**: Clean, professional sans-serif fonts (Inter for body, Poppins for headings)
- **Design Tokens**: Consistent spacing, shadows, and border radius for medical/clinical feel
- **Responsive Grid**: Mobile-first design with breakpoints for all devices

### Animation Framework
- **GSAP Integration**: For smooth scroll animations, text reveals, and image transitions
- **Three.js Setup**: 3D background elements (floating particles/molecules for medical feel)
- **Framer Motion**: For micro-interactions and component animations
